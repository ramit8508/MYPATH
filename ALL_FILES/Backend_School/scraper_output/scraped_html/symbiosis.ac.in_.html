Symbiosis International (Deemed University) is ranked 200th in Asia, 34th in Southern Asia, and 20th in India in the QS World University Asia Rankings 2026. The University has once again proven its academic excellence and global presence with remarkable achievements in this year’s university rankings.

A surgical team at Symbiosis University Hospital and Research Centre (SUHRC), led by Dr. Kalpesh Patil, successfully performed a rare and delicate operation on a 10-year-old girl suffering from Rapunzel Syndrome (compulsive hair swallowing). The intricate, 2.5 hour surgery removed a 280 gram mass of hair and thread (trichobezoar) from the patient's stomach, and the girl is recovering well post-procedure, highlighting SUHRC's expertise in complex pediatric surgery.

Symbiosis Centre for Health Skills (SCHS) has been honoured with the ASPiH Accreditation Award at the Association for Simulated Practice in Healthcare (ASPiH) Conference 2025, held in Bournemouth, United Kingdom. This international recognition marks a significant milestone for SCHS, acknowledging its unwavering commitment to excellence in simulation-based healthcare education and patient safety training.

Symbiosis International (Deemed University) celebrates a significant milestone with the inclusion of 20 of its distinguished faculty members in the prestigious Stanford University's Top 2% Scientists List.

Symbiosis has been synonymous with quality education for over 50 years. Drawing strength from this trust, ‘Symbiosis University Hospital and Research Centre’ (SUHRC) was conceived to redefine healthcare in Pune by establishing a new state-of-the-art tertiary care hospital. Our purpose is to provide modern & professional healthcare to the Rural & Urban community equitably.

SUHRC started operations in mid-2019, and boasts a strong team of distinguished healthcare practitioners, qualified nursing and paramedical staff. The Medical team of the hospital comprises doctors who are among the best, all trained in leading hospitals in India and abroad. The hospital has the latest, state-of-the-art technology to assist the doctors. The hospital’s focus is on research and it has collaborated with the existing research centres of the University.

Symbiosis Literary Festival (SLF) 2025

Symbiosis International (Deemed University) (SIU) hosted the sixth edition of the Symbiosis Literary Festival (SLF) 2025. Organized by the Symbiosis School for Liberal Arts (SSLA), the festival brought together a diverse group of thinkers, artists, and creators for a vibrant celebration of ideas. This year's theme, "Ink, Ideas and Inspiration," set the stage for engaging discussions spanning literature, cinema, art, and intellectual thought.
Dr. Shashi Tharoor, Hon’ble Member of Parliament (Lok Sabha) & Chairman of the Parliamentary Standing Committee of External Affairs, graced the occasion as the Chief Guest. The Book '200 Motivational Mondays' authored by Dr. Vidya Yeravdekar, Pro Chancellor, SIU was released at the hands of the Chief Guest Dr. Shashi Tharoor, Hon’ble Member of Parliament (Lok Sabha) & Chairman of the Parliamentary Standing Committee of External Affairs during the Symbiosis Literary Festival (SLF) 2025 . The SLF 2025 continues its tradition of fostering intellectual discourse and celebrating the power of storytelling and creativity within the university community and beyond.

'Legendary Institution Builder Award 2025' Conferred on Prof (Dr.) S.B. Mujumdar, Chancellor, SIU

FICCI conferred Prof (Dr.) S.B. Mujumdar, Founder and President, Symbiosis Chancellor, Symbiosis International (Deemed University) with the prestigious 'Legendary Institution Builder Award 2025' at the 20th FICCI Higher Education Summit and Awards 2025.

This honour celebrates his visionary leadership and lifelong commitment to nation-building through education. From a humble beginning, Symbiosis has grown under his guidance into a globally respected university known for academic excellence, inclusivity, and innovation.

LEADERSHIP

Prof. (Dr.) Shantaram Balwant Mujumdar (Founder & President)

Dr. S.B. Mujumdar is the Founder and President of Symbiosis – a multi-national, multi-lingual and multidisciplinary educational complex of repute. A distinguished academician and educationist, he is also the Chancellor of Symbiosis International (Deemed University).


Dr. Mujumdar was born on 31st July 1935 at Gadhinglaj, a Taluka in Kolhapur District in Maharashtra. He had his school education at Gadhinglaj and college education at Kolhapur and Pune. In his Master's Degree (M.Sc.) in Botany, he stood first class first with distinction in Pune University. He obtained his Doctorate (Ph.D.) in Microbiology from Pune University.


He joined Fergusson College, Pune as Professor and Head of Department in Botany which position he held for 20 years. He was a recognized post graduate teacher and a guide for Ph.D. students. He was a member of the Pune University's Executive Council, the Senate, Academic Council (for 14 years) and Chairman of the Board of Studies in Botany (for 9 years). He was also I.C.C.R's Foreign Academic Advisor in Pune University (for 7 years). He has published over 50 original research articles in several national and international scientific journals. He has authored several books on Life Sciences and has contributed over 200 articles on Science, Education and Youth Development. He has also been the Chairman of the Educational Wing of FICCI in 2005-2006.
