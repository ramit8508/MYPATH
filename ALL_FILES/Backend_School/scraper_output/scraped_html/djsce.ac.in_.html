Affiliations & Accreditations

DJSCE is an Autonomous Institution, granted autonomy by the University Grants Commission (UGC) for a period of 10 years, starting from the A.Y. 2019-20 till 2028-29.
The National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) has accredited DJSCE with a CGPA of 3.18 out of 4, i.e., with an A Grade for a period of 5 years.
The college runs 8 undergraduate programs, 3 post graduate programmes and 3 PhD programmes for the A.Y. 2024-25, all permanently affiliated to the University of Mumbai.
University of Mumbai, in addition to granting autonomy has also granted Empowered Autonomous Status for a period of 10 years, till the A.Y. 2033-34.
