Industry-Collaborated
Centres of Excellence

KCC Lab

The KCCRSST is giving satellite design training to students of 21 countries. The Space Centre can communicate with 28 countries that are part of Satellite Networked Open Ground Station (SatNOGS).

Eicher-CU Centre of Excellence

The centre offers live workshops by industry experts, hands-on practice for students, and research on blended fuel efficiency, BS-VI commercial heavy diesel engines, and BS-VI engine emissions control techniques.

CoE-Ultratech Lab

The CoE aims to drive research and development, facilitate industry-academia collaboration, enhance skill development, and create pathways for student placements in the construction and materials sector.

Coforge Lab

Innovation center responsible for developing solutions for real-world problems at national and international levels, advancing autonomous vehicles, and conducting R&D on AI applications in healthcare.

LIVE Kitchen

Our LIVE Kitchen serves as a dynamic learning space where culinary creativity meets professional expertise. Designed like an industry-standard kitchen, it offers students experiential training in food production, presentation, and modern gastronomy techniques, preparing them to excel in hospitality and global culinary arts.

Electrical Engineering Lab

Equipped with advanced simulation tools and modern equipment, our Electrical Engineering Lab provides hands-on training in power systems, circuit design, control systems, and renewable energy applications. It bridges theoretical concepts with real-world applications, preparing students to innovate in the ever-evolving electrical and electronics industry.

M.E. Workshop

The Mechanical Engineering Workshop nurtures innovation through practical exposure to machining, fabrication, welding, and prototyping. Students gain first-hand experience in core mechanical processes, supported by state-of-the-art tools and machines, ensuring readiness for both industrial challenges and cutting-edge research.

Media Studios

Our Media Studios are designed to immerse students in the fast-paced world of journalism and digital media. With industry-standard equipment for news production, editing, and broadcasting, these studios provide hands-on training that fosters storytelling, creativity, and ethical reporting across multiple platforms.

Moot Court

The Moot Court replicates real courtroom settings, allowing law students to sharpen advocacy, argumentation, and legal reasoning skills. By engaging in simulated trials, debates, and case studies, it cultivates confidence, professionalism, and practical exposure to the judicial system.
