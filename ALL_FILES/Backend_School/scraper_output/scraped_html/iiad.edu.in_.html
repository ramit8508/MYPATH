Art design

Evolve your own creative process through our unique pedagogy in a world-class learning environment

We inculcate an inquiry based approach to learning, enabling you to develop awareness of yourself and the world. This inspires you to delve deep and discover your individual expression.

world class partnerships

We have an academic association with Kingston University, London which has been ranked as the #1 Art & Design school in London by The Times Good University Guide 2023. The globally-recognised course enables our students to secure coveted career as well as academic opportunities across the globe.

Additionally, we have also built association with other leading universities such as University for the Creative Arts, Nottingham Trent University, Coventry University, Arts University Bournemouth and many more. Such associations enable student transfers and exchange programmes globally.

Graduate with a resume, ready for the professional world
