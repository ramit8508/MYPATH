    • Modern Campus Facilities

      GJUST's modern campus provides students with well-equipped classrooms, advanced laboratories, and an extensive library. These facilities support a conducive learning environment, allowing students to explore their academic interests effectively.

    • Research and Innovation Opportunities

      GJUST encourages students to actively engage in research and innovation. Collaborative projects with faculty, participation in conferences, and access to cutting-edge technology contribute to a culture of intellectual exploration and creativity

    • Extracurricular Engagement

      Beyond academics, students have the opportunity to participate in a variety of extracurricular activities. Sports events, cultural festivals, and student clubs offer platforms for personal growth, skill development, and social interaction, fostering a well-rounded student experience.

    Research & Innovation

    Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam Central Instrumentation Laboratory is a well acclaimed laboratory which not only provides facilities to researchers of the University but also caters to the research needs of other Institutions of the region. The University has well sought-after Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya Innovation and Incubation Centre and Start-Up Schemes to foster innovative ideas of its students.
