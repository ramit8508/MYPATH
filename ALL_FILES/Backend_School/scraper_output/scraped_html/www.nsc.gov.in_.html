Who We Are

National Seeds Corporation Ltd. (NSC) is a Schedule ‘B’-Miniratna Category-I company wholly owned by Government of India under the administrative control of Department of Agriculture & Farmer’s Welfare, Ministry of Agriculture and Farmer's Welfare. NSC was established in March-1963 to undertake production of Foundation and Certified Seeds.
